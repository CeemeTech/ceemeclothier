HOW IT ALL STARTED

Ceeme Clothiers was originally conceptualized as an online store but ultimately a physical store was opened instead in 2010 and has been open since then.

Ceeme Clothiers was founded by Oluremi SimileJesu aka Ceeme who is the ultimate fashion enthusiast.

His interest, and passion for fashion trends, and style is unparalleled and dates far back as when he was in high school.

He studied Music at the Mount Allison University Canada and obtained a Master’s degree in Musical Composition at the same University.

However, He couldn’t resist what would appear to be his passion and immediately launched Ceeme Clothiers Flagship store after returning home to Lagos from Canada.

Since then, he has built Ceeme Clothiers to be a reputable and sought after for both Men and women’s clothing store in Lagos Nigeria.

If there is that event/occasion be it a Wedding, concert, dinner date, clubbing etc. C.C today is one of the top destinations for men and women ages 16-45 to find that befitting outfit worthy of the occasion in Lagos.

He has since launched another store in Accra, Ghana December 2019, and C.C has quickly risen to become the “Goto” in Accra today.
